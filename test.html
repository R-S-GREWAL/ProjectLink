The following tests are to check the symptoms for the most common childhood Cancers :

Note: Your Symptoms can be different from the common symptoms of the following cancers, It is advised not to trust the following tests completely.

Leukemia

Do you get frequent fevers and chills?




Have you suddenly lost weight without trying?




Do you get Frequent and severe infections?




Are you experiencing excessive sweating, especially at night?




Are you experiencing frequent bleeding and bruising?




Instructions


If you have one or two symptoms there is no need to worry!

If you have three or more symptoms you should consider a Doctor...

If you have five or more symptoms you should definately consult a doctor.



Brain Tumor

Are you having Eye problems, such as blurry vision, seeing double or losing sight on the sides of your vision ?




Are you lately having trouble in Maintaining Balance?




Do headaches happen more often and seem more severe?




Do you have memory problems, like having trouble in remembering things?




Are you experiencing problems in speech?




Do you always feel tired without an obvious reason?




Do you experience Losing movement in an arm or a leg?
