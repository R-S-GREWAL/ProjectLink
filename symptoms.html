Common Symptoms of Childhood Cancer




It can be very hard to detect cancer in children. Children with cancer can experience a variety of the symptoms
of which many symptoms are similar to common childhood illness. Symptoms are changes that you can feel in
your body. Sometimes, children with cancer do not have any of the common symptoms mentioned below Or
the cause of a symptom may be of a medical condition that is not the Cancer.
The following are the possible concerning symptoms and signs of Childhood Cancer to talk to the doctor about :






  • Unusual Lump and Swelling
  • Unexplained Paleness
  • Loss of Energy without a reason
  • Having long-lasting Fever
  • Sudden Weight Loss
  • Sudden Vision Changes
  • Nausea and Vomiting
  • Frequent and Constant Infections
  • A Whitish Colour behind the Pupil
  • Excessive Bleeding and Rashes
